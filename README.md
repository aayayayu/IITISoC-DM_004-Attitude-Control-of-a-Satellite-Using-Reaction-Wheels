# IITISoC-DM_004-Attitude-Control-of-a-Satellite-Using-Reaction-Wheels
## ADCS for LEO Cubesats
The aim of this project is to design a realistically feasible cubesat and reaction wheels and then implement and simulate the ADCS loop for two cases:
-To go from an arbitary orientation to desired orientation and maintain that
-To recover from unexpected external disturbances
##Reaction wheels 
Reaction wheels are entirely internal, consume very little power, and offer extremely fine pointing control. They are the preferred choice for missions demanding high accuracy — from large space telescopes to small student-built CubeSats.
